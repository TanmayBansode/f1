# F1 Season Visualizer

[podiums.dev](https://podiums.dev)

This application provides an interactive way to follow and analyze the  Formula 1 seasons. It uses dynamic charts to show how driver standings change after every Qualifying, Sprint, and Grand Prix session.

### Usecases

*   **Track Championship Standings:** View the progression of the driver's championship across the entire season.
*   **Driver Comparison:** Highlight specific drivers to compare their performance and ranking trends against the rest of the grid.
*   **Session Analysis:** Filter results to see how different types of sessions (Qualifying vs. Races) impact the overall standings.
*   **Historical & Future Data:** Access and explore full result sets for both the 2025 and 2026 seasons.
*   **Detailed Results:** Access specific race data, including points earned, final positions, and team information by interacting with the season timeline.
